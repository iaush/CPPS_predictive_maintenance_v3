How It Works

Problem

The state-of-the-art in a posteriori multi-objective optimization does not explicitly handle uncertainty. Hence it is often too slow for such problems

Capabilities

Memetic Computing
Integrating global evolutionary search with local search in a learned low dimensional subspace for faster convergence.

Transfer and Multi-task Optimization
Transfer and adaptive reuse of knowledge from related uncertain conditions to speedup the target optimization search.

Benefits

  1. Highly scalable and parallelizable
  2. Supports big uncertainty data
  3. No assumption about functional forms of objectives/uncertainties
