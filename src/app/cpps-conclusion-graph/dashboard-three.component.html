The CPPS WP3.3 algorithm utilizes deep reinforcement learning to recommend prescriptive actions when an incoming failure is detected. The advantages of said algorithm includes:
  1. Suggestion of prescriptive actions to rectify incoming failure which traditional predictive maintenance methodology are unable to do so

  2. Optimized timing for maintenance action which attempts to determine the best time for maintenance action to be taken. This lowers maintenance cost by up to 28% compared to other state-of-the-art algorithms

  3. Faster training speed for deep reinforcement learning model with reductions in training time by up to 8% compared to other state-of-the-art algorithms
