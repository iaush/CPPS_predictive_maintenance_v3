Conclusion

System level prescriptive maintenance

System level prescriptive maintenance algorithm which not only predicts the degradation level but also determines root cause of the system failure to facilitate the decision of the maintenance actions.

By integration with domain knowledge, the proposed algorithm outperformed the state of art by around 10% to 30% in F1 Score root cause identification.

Offline deep reinforcement learning

Offline deep reinforcement learning algorithm to recommend maintenance actions to optimize the total cost.

Minimum total cost. CPPS WP3.3 algorithm recommends the optimal action sequence, which helps minimize the total cost by around 18% to 45% compared with the state-of-the-art algorithms.

Reduce training time. CPPS WP3.3 algorithm leverages the historical data for training. The training time for CPPS WP3.3 algorithm is 7% shorter than the state-of-the-art algorithm.
